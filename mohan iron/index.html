About Us

About us

Mohan Iron & Steel Trading Company Ltd. (GISCO) is a leading trading and distribution house in various Iron, mild steel and special steel products. Our company established in year 1991 at Nagpur and another leap forward expanded to pan India to serve our customers with branches and stockyards at Nagpur, Mumbai, Pune, Bhilai, Ahmadabad, and Durgapur & Vishakhapatnam.

About Us

About us

We are the authorized dealer of SAIL/RINL/JSPL/Essar/Electro steel/Jindal Rolling products & distributor of BLUE LINES TMT for entire Maharashtra state having a channel presence with more than 200 retailers. Our every stockyard is fully modernize with over head cranes, adequate stocks to fulfill your needs along with highly experienced Technical manpower to cater you uninterrupted and timely deliveries.
